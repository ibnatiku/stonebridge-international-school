Admission Process

1. Inquiry

Contact our admission office or fill the inquiry form online.

2. Entrance Exam

Child undergoes assessment to determine the appropriate level.

3. Interview

Parent & child meet with our academic team to discuss expectations.

4. Enrollment

Submit all documents, pay fees, and officially secure your child’s place.

Requirements

  • Birth certificate or age verification
  • Previous school records (if applicable)
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Medical records and immunization certificate
