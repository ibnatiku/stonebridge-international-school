Code of Conduct

Our standards guide students, parents, and staff to maintain a respectful and safe environment.

Our Key Policies

Respect & Responsibility

All students and staff are expected to treat others with respect and take responsibility for their actions.

Attendance & Punctuality

Regular attendance and punctuality are mandatory for all students. Exceptions require prior notification.

Dress Code

Students must adhere to the school uniform policy at all times while on campus.

Academic Integrity

Cheating, plagiarism, or dishonest behavior in any form will not be tolerated.

Safety & Wellbeing

The school maintains a safe environment and encourages reporting of unsafe conditions or behavior.
